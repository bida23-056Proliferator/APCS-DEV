Privacy Policy

Effective date: 1 January 2025 · Last updated:

1. Introduction

Alpha Pack Cash Solutions ("Alpha Pack," "we," "us," or "our") operates the website and student lending platform (the "Platform"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you use our Platform. By registering an account or submitting any information through the Platform, you consent to the practices described in this Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information directly from you:

✓ Account data: Full name, email address, phone number, institution, programme of study, year of study, and Student ID number — provided during registration.
✓ Verification documents: Photographs of the front and back of your Student ID card — required to verify your student status.
✓ Loan application data: Loan amount requested, purpose of loan, repayment preference, and contact phone number — collected when you apply.
✓ Usage data: Pages visited, timestamps of logins, and interactions with the Platform — collected automatically for security and analytics.
3. How We Use Your Information
▸To verify your identity as a currently enrolled student at a recognised institution.
▸To process, review, approve, or decline your loan applications.
▸To communicate with you via email or WhatsApp regarding your account status, application outcome, and repayment obligations.
▸To maintain platform security, prevent fraud, and audit admin actions.
▸To comply with applicable laws and regulatory obligations in Botswana.
4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase infrastructure, with encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest. Student ID photographs are stored in a private, access-controlled storage bucket. Only authorised Alpha Pack administrators can view your verification documents — and only for the purpose of verifying your student status. We implement strict Row Level Security (RLS) policies to ensure you can only access your own data.

5. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may disclose information only in the following circumstances:

▸To authorised Alpha Pack staff members who require access to perform their duties (KYC review, loan processing).
▸If required by law, court order, or governmental regulation in Botswana.
▸To protect the rights, property, or safety of Alpha Pack, our users, or the public — including fraud prevention.
6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account remains active or as needed to provide you with our services. Specifically:

▸Active accounts: Data retained indefinitely while your account is active.
▸Closed accounts: Personal data may be retained for up to 5 years for legal, audit, and regulatory compliance.
▸Verification photos: Stored for the lifetime of your account plus 12 months after closure, then permanently deleted.
7. Your Rights

As a registered user, you have the right to:

▸Access and review the personal data we hold about you — viewable directly in your dashboard.
▸Request correction of inaccurate information by contacting our support team.
▸Request deletion of your account and associated data — subject to legal retention obligations.
▸Withdraw consent at any time, where processing is based on consent, without affecting prior lawful processing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via WhatsApp or email using the details in Section 9.

8. Cookies & Tracking

Alpha Pack uses essential session tokens for authentication (managed by Supabase Auth). We do not use third-party tracking cookies, analytics pixels, or advertising trackers on our Platform. The Platform functions fully without non-essential cookies.
